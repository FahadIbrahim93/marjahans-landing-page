# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Marjahans Jewellery e-commerce platform is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and professional environment for all contributors, team members, and collaborators. We are dedicated to creating a space where everyone feels respected, valued, and empowered to contribute their best work.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants in this project to uphold the following standards:

### Professional Behavior

All team members and contributors should conduct themselves professionally at all times. This includes being respectful in all interactions, whether in code reviews, issues, pull requests, or discussions. We value diverse perspectives and encourage healthy debate about technical decisions, but all communication should remain constructive and focused on the work.

### Inclusive Environment

We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where all individuals feel welcome and valued. We do not tolerate discrimination based on age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, political beliefs, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or any other characteristic.

### Respectful Communication

When communicating with other team members and contributors, we expect:

- **Respectful language** - Use professional and courteous language at all times
- **Active listening** - Listen to understand, not just to respond
- **Constructive feedback** - Provide feedback that is helpful and aimed at improvement
- **Assumption of good intent** - Assume others are trying their best and acting in good faith
- **Prompt responses** - Respond to questions and feedback in a timely manner
- **Acknowledgment of mistakes** - Admit errors and work to correct them

### Professional Excellence

We are committed to maintaining high standards of professional excellence:

- **Quality work** - Produce code and documentation that meets our standards
- **Continuous improvement** - Seek to improve your skills and knowledge
- **Knowledge sharing** - Help others learn and grow
- **Accountability** - Take responsibility for your work and commitments
- **Timeliness** - Meet deadlines and communicate delays promptly
- **Attention to detail** - Pay careful attention to quality and accuracy

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- **Be professional** - Conduct yourself professionally in all interactions
- **Be respectful** - Treat all team members and contributors with respect
- **Be collaborative** - Work together toward shared goals
- **Be helpful** - Assist others when they need help
- **Be honest** - Be truthful in all communications and work
- **Be accountable** - Take responsibility for your actions and decisions
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome and support people from all backgrounds
- **Be patient** - Understand that people work at different paces
- **Be constructive** - Focus on solutions, not problems
- **Be professional** - Maintain professional standards in all work

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- **Harassment** - Any form of harassment based on protected characteristics
- **Discrimination** - Treating people unfairly based on their characteristics
- **Bullying** - Intimidating, threatening, or abusive behavior
- **Disrespect** - Being disrespectful or rude to team members
- **Exclusion** - Deliberately excluding people from discussions or decisions
- **Unprofessional conduct** - Behavior that violates professional standards
- **Violation of privacy** - Sharing private information without consent
- **Conflict of interest** - Failing to disclose conflicts of interest
- **Dishonesty** - Lying or being deceptive
- **Sabotage** - Deliberately undermining others' work or the project

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it immediately to the project lead. When reporting a violation:

1. **Be specific** - Provide details about what happened
2. **Include context** - Explain the circumstances and impact
3. **Provide evidence** - Include any relevant messages or documentation
4. **Be honest** - Be truthful in your report
5. **Maintain confidentiality** - Keep the report confidential

All reports will be taken seriously and investigated promptly. We are committed to protecting the privacy of those who report violations and will not tolerate retaliation against anyone who reports in good faith.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct will be addressed promptly and fairly. Depending on the severity and nature of the violation, consequences may include:

1. **Verbal warning** - For minor violations
2. **Written warning** - For more serious violations
3. **Temporary suspension** - For repeated violations
4. **Permanent removal** - For severe or repeated violations

The project lead will determine appropriate consequences based on the specific circumstances.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the Marjahans Jewellery project, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Code reviews and comments
- Commit messages
- Project documentation
- Any official project communications

This Code of Conduct also applies to official project events and activities, whether online or in-person.

## Specific Guidelines for Different Contexts

### Code Reviews

During code reviews, we expect:

- **Constructive feedback** - Focus on the code, not the person
- **Respectful tone** - Use professional and courteous language
- **Specific comments** - Provide specific, actionable feedback
- **Appreciation** - Thank reviewers for their time and effort
- **Responsiveness** - Respond to feedback promptly

### Issue Discussions

When discussing issues, we expect:

- **Respectful debate** - Disagree respectfully about technical decisions
- **Focus on the issue** - Keep discussion focused on the problem at hand
- **Evidence-based** - Support your arguments with facts and evidence
- **Open-minded** - Be willing to consider other perspectives
- **Constructive** - Focus on finding solutions

### Pull Request Discussions

When discussing pull requests, we expect:

- **Professional tone** - Maintain a professional tone in all comments
- **Constructive feedback** - Provide feedback aimed at improvement
- **Timely responses** - Respond to feedback promptly
- **Appreciation** - Thank contributors for their work
- **Respect for effort** - Acknowledge the effort put into the work

## Commitment to Improvement

We are committed to continuously improving our community and culture. We welcome feedback on this Code of Conduct and are open to suggestions for improvement. If you have ideas for how we can create a better environment, please share them with the project lead.

## Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and other community codes of conduct. We are grateful for the work of those who have created these resources and are committed to upholding similar standards in our community.

## Questions

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct or need clarification on any part of it, please reach out to the project lead. We are here to help ensure that everyone understands and can uphold these standards.
