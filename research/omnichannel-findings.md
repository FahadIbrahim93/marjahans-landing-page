# Omnichannel Retail Strategy Research - Marjahan's Jewelry

## Key Findings from Research

### 1. Omnichannel Retail Definition & Benefits
- **Unified Experience**: Customers can start shopping on one channel and finish on another seamlessly
- **Consistent Data**: Same pricing, promotions, inventory visibility across all touchpoints
- **Higher Conversion**: 35% conversion rate achieved by brands using clienteling programs
- **Customer Retention**: 91% of retail consumers prefer omnichannel experiences
- **77% of shoppers** abandon purchases if there's too much friction

### 2. Critical Components for Success
- **Integrated Data Systems**: Real-time inventory sync across all channels
- **Unified Customer View**: CDP (Customer Data Platform) combining all touchpoints
- **Consistent Pricing & Promotions**: Across Facebook, Instagram, Shopify, TikTok Shop
- **Seamless Checkout**: Buy online, pick up in store (BOPIS) capabilities
- **Real-time Inventory Tracking**: IoT and automated sync systems

### 3. Shopify-Facebook Integration Status
- **Automatic Sync**: Shopify automatically syncs products to Facebook every 7 days
- **Manual Sync Available**: Force manual updates in Meta Commerce Manager
- **Data Sources**: Shopify acts as master data source for product information
- **Inventory Sync**: Stock levels sync automatically (when properly configured)
- **Common Issues**: 
  - Inventory not syncing properly
  - Catalog filters preventing product visibility
  - Manual sync needed for immediate updates

### 4. Meta Business Manager Capabilities
- **Commerce Manager**: Central hub for managing products across Facebook/Instagram
- **Web Crawling**: Meta can auto-sync products from website
- **Multiple Integration Methods**:
  - Shopify direct integration (recommended)
  - Custom data feeds
  - Web crawling (limited to eligible businesses)
- **Catalog Filters**: Can restrict product visibility if misconfigured

### 5. Current Technology Landscape (2026)
- **5G Networks**: Enable real-time inventory updates and AR/VR experiences
- **Augmented Reality**: Virtual try-on for jewelry products
- **Voice Commerce**: Voice assistant integration for product discovery
- **AI Chatbots**: Automated customer service across channels
- **IoT Devices**: Real-time inventory tracking

## Why Products Not Showing

### Potential Issues:
1. **Shopify-Facebook Connection Not Fully Activated**
   - App installed but not properly configured
   - Domain verification incomplete
   - Checkout method not selected

2. **Catalog Filters Blocking Products**
   - Products filtered out in Meta Commerce Manager
   - Category restrictions applied
   - Inventory filters set to hide out-of-stock items

3. **Sync Timing Issues**
   - Shopify syncs every 7 days (not real-time)
   - Manual sync needed for immediate updates
   - First sync may take 24-48 hours

4. **Data Mapping Issues**
   - Product fields not properly mapped
   - Missing required fields (title, price, image, description)
   - Inventory field not connected

5. **Meta Business Manager Configuration**
   - Catalog not properly linked to shop
   - Data source not set to Shopify
   - Shop not published to Facebook/Instagram

## Jewelry-Specific Omnichannel Strategy for Marjahan's

### Market Opportunity
- Global jewelry market projected to grow from $254.13B (2026) to $387.36B (2034) at 5.41% CAGR
- Demand for high-end pieces up 12%, average spend rising by double digits
- Omnichannel customers spend 4% more in-store and 10% more online than single-channel shoppers

### Key Success Factors for Jewelry Retail

**1. Visual Confidence & Virtual Try-On**
- Customers prioritize visual confidence over physical touch (2026 trend)
- Professional 360-degree product photography essential
- AR virtual try-on technology critical for jewelry (see how pieces look on them)
- High-resolution images with pinch-to-zoom for mobile viewing
- Short-form video content for Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest

**2. Mobile-First Optimization**
- Mobile accounts for majority of initial jewelry discovery
- Mobile page speed critical (Google Page Experience)
- One-click checkout and digital wallet integration (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Local SEO optimization ("jewelry stores near me" searches)
- Graduated engagement: reserve items, schedule consultations via mobile

**3. Data Integration & Inventory Sync**
- Real-time inventory visibility across all channels
- Vendor-managed inventory integration (JewelCloud model)
- EDI partnerships for automatic product data exchange
- Customer data unified in CRM across all touchpoints
- AI-powered analytics for purchase pattern recognition

**4. Trust & Brand Storytelling**
- Consistent brand voice across all channels
- Professional lifestyle photography showing pieces in context
- Educational content about gemstones, materials, design trends
- Customer testimonials and user-generated content
- Reputation management and review monitoring
- Expert-driven customer service across all channels

**5. Seamless Digital-to-In-Store Experience**
- Customers add items to wishlist online → staff sees in-store
- In-store appointments booked online → team has browsing history
- Personal shopper video calls for online customers
- QR codes in showroom linking to digital product information
- High-value consultation focus (not just browsing)

### Technology Stack Requirements

1. **E-commerce Platform**: Shopify (already connected)

3. **Inventory System**: Real-time sync across Shopify, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok
4. **CRM System**: Unified customer data platform

6. **AR/VR**: Virtual try-on technology for jewelry
7. **Communication**: Email, SMS, WhatsApp, Messenger integration
